Press any arrow key to start.

Doorstep

You stand outside a house.
A door is in front of you.
There is no turning back now.

Use your arrow keys to move forward. (Up)

Ground Floor Foyer

You are in the foyer.
The vast house extends in every direction.
In front of you, the stairwell rises to the second floor.

Use Page Up to climb the stairs.

Dining Room

The dining table appears recently set.
Missing, however, are the diners.

Surrounded by windows, your only option is to return east to the foyer.

Hallway

Along the interior wall of the hallway tapestries depict the history of the family.
The outer wall of the hose is lined with windows overlooking the grounds.

Maid Station

Tucked behind the grand staircase you find a small alcove where the maid should be on duty.
She is not.

Library

You enter the library, the boards squeak under your feet.
A layer of dust coats most surfaces.

First Floor

You stand atop the stairwell.
Nothing to see here.

Use Page Down to descend the stairs.

*crack*

Arrrggghhhh...

Basement

The splintered boards of the hole you just fell through litter the floor.
You appear trapped.

...and that is where the story ends.
